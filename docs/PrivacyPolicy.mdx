Your privacy matters to us. Casbin respects your privacy regarding any information collected from you through our [documentation website](/) and other sites we operate.

We request personal information only when necessary to provide services. We collect information fairly and lawfully, with your knowledge and consent. We explain why we need the information and how we will use it.

We retain collected information only as long as needed to deliver the requested service. We protect stored data using commercially acceptable methods to prevent loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

We do not share personally identifying information publicly or with third parties, except when legally required.

Our website may contain links to external sites we do not operate. We have no control over these sites' content and practices and cannot accept responsibility for their privacy policies.

You may refuse our request for personal information, understanding this may prevent us from providing certain services.

Continued use of our website indicates acceptance of our privacy and personal information practices. Contact us with any questions about how we handle user data and personal information.

This policy became effective on 29th June 2020.
